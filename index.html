About

Yazi Cole began her journey in professional modeling by winning her first pageant in Switzerland. This launched her modeling career and paved her way to success all throughout Europe. She made her way to Miami, Florida where she walked for Miami and Fort Lauderdale Fashion Week, picked up a shoot for Sephora, and made it as a top 10 finalists for a hit singing show called, “Making The Next Hit with Lil Wayne.” Continuing her profession, she journeyed across Asia, where she amassed a ton of opportunities in showbiz. Being featured in numerous TV commercials, Magazines, TV shows, and Billboards caught the eye of many popular brands. She nailed deals with the industries top brands such as Levis, MAC cosmetics, Versace, and Mossimo just to name a few. Yazi is still proactive after many years in the industry. She now resides in Hollywood, California where she’s focused on becoming the next big influencer.
